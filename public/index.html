Who We Are

Committed to Excellence in Commodity Trading

Founded in 2010, BNB Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. is a registered member of the Multi Commodity Exchange of India (MCX), regulated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI).

For over a decade, we have been committed to delivering transparent, technology-driven, and research-backed commodity trading services. Our strength lies in a team of experienced market professionals whose combined expertise spans more than 50 years across domestic and international commodity markets, including India, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Malaysia.

Beyond broking, our deep involvement in proprietary trading and market research enables us to fulfil our client requirements. At BNB Financial Services, we combine market knowledge, disciplined execution, and a client-first approach to build lasting relationships founded on trust and performance.
